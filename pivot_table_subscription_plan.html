Conclusion

The data shows that customers subscribing to the Premium plan tend to have the highest satisfaction ratings across all regions, followed by Standard and Basic plans. Rural regions generally have slightly lower satisfaction ratings compared to Urban and Semi-Urban regions.

Recommendations

1. Focus on Premium Plans: Promote the Premium subscription plan as it delivers the highest satisfaction ratings. Highlight its benefits in marketing campaigns.

2. Improve Basic Plan Satisfaction: Address gaps in the Basic plan by enhancing features or offering better value.

3. Region-Specific Strategies: Tailor subscription features or offers for Rural regions to improve satisfaction levels.

4. Encourage Upgrades: Provide incentives for customers on Basic or Standard plans to upgrade to Premium for increased satisfaction and revenue.
