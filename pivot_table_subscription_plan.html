Conclusion

The data shows that customers subscribing to the Premium plan tend to have the highest satisfaction ratings across all regions, followed by Standard and Basic plans. Rural regions generally have slightly lower satisfaction ratings compared to Urban and Semi-Urban regions.

Recommendations

1. Focus on Premium Plans: Promote the Premium subscription plan as it delivers the highest satisfaction ratings. Highlight its benefits to customers in marketing campaigns.

2. Improve Basic Plan Satisfaction: Investigate the reasons for lower satisfaction ratings in the Basic plan and address gaps by enhancing features or offering better value.

3. Region-Specific Strategies: Tailor subscription features or offers to address the specific needs of Rural regions, as their satisfaction levels are generally lower.

4. Encourage Upgrades: Provide incentives for customers on Basic or Standard plans to upgrade to Premium to increase satisfaction and revenue.
