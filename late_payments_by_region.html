Working

The raw data was processed to identify late payments by comparing the actual payment date with the expected payment date. Only payments made in the last six months were included in this analysis.

Conclusions

  • Semi-Urban: Highest late payments suggest a need for better engagement and follow-up.
  • Urban and Rural: Equal late payments indicate possible systemic issues in these areas.

Recommendations

  • Implement automated reminders to reduce late payments in Semi-Urban areas.
  • Analyze Urban and Rural areas for specific barriers causing late payments.
  • Leverage strategies from successful regions to improve outcomes in high-late-payment areas.
