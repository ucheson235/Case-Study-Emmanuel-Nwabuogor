Conclusion

The data reveals that Rural regions have the highest number of late payments, followed by Urban regions, with Semi-Urban regions showing the lowest number. This trend suggests that customers in Rural areas face more challenges in meeting payment deadlines, potentially due to service accessibility or financial constraints.

Recommendations

1. Provide personalized reminders, payment flexibility, or incentives to encourage on-time payments in Rural regions.

2. Launch financial education campaigns in regions with high late payments to improve awareness.

3. Ensure Rural regions have easy access to payment platforms, such as mobile apps or nearby agents.

4. Maintain proactive support and incentives for Urban regions to further reduce late payments.
