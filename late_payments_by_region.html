Conclusion

The data reveals that Rural regions have the highest number of late payments, followed by Urban regions, with Semi-Urban regions showing the lowest number. This trend suggests that customers in Rural areas face more challenges in meeting payment deadlines, potentially due to service accessibility or financial constraints.

Recommendations

1. Targeted Support for Rural Customers: Provide personalized reminders, payment flexibility, or incentives to encourage on-time payments.

2. Financial Education Campaigns: Launch campaigns in regions with high late payments to improve awareness of the benefits of timely payments.

3. Enhanced Accessibility: Ensure Rural regions have easy access to payment platforms, such as mobile apps or nearby agents.

4. Monitor Urban Late Payments: Although Urban regions have fewer late payments than Rural regions, maintaining proactive support and incentives will help reduce these further.
