Conclusion

The data shows that Urban regions have the highest average satisfaction ratings, followed by Semi-Urban and Rural regions. Rural regions consistently show lower satisfaction levels, indicating potential service gaps or unmet needs in these areas.

Recommendations

1. Focus on Rural Regions: Conduct detailed surveys or focus groups in Rural areas to identify key drivers of dissatisfaction and address them proactively.

2. Enhance Service Quality:Introduce targeted programs or benefits in Rural regions to boost customer satisfaction.

3. Leverage Urban Success: Use successful practices from Urban regions as a blueprint for improving customer satisfaction in other regions.

4. Consistent Monitoring: Regularly track satisfaction ratings across all regions to identify and address issues promptly.
