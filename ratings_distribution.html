Conclusion

The data reveals a significant proportion of customers rating their satisfaction in the range of 6–8, indicating moderate satisfaction. However, a noticeable percentage of ratings fall below 5, suggesting dissatisfaction among a portion of the customer base.

Recommendations

1. Improve Service Quality: Focus on customers rating their satisfaction below 5 by identifying pain points and addressing them proactively.

2. Engage Moderately Satisfied Customers: Target customers with ratings of 6–8 with incentives or improved services to elevate their satisfaction.

3. Maintain High Satisfaction Levels: Continue offering premium services and personalized experiences to customers rating 9–10 to retain loyalty.

4. Collect Detailed Feedback: Regularly collect detailed feedback from low-rating customers to understand and mitigate dissatisfaction.
