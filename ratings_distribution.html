Conclusion

The data reveals a significant proportion of customers rating their satisfaction in the range of 6–8, indicating moderate satisfaction. However, a noticeable percentage of ratings fall below 5, suggesting dissatisfaction among a portion of the customer base.

Recommendations

1. Improve Service Quality: Address pain points for customers rating below 5.

2. Engage Moderately Satisfied Customers: Incentivize ratings of 6–8.

3. Maintain High Satisfaction Levels: Retain loyalty of ratings 9–10.

4. Collect Detailed Feedback: Regularly gather feedback to improve services.
